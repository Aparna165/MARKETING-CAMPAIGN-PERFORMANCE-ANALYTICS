## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing marketing campaign performance across multiple dimensions such as **channels, demographics, and time**.

The dashboard helps stakeholders:
✔ Understand campaign effectiveness  
✔ Compare ROI across channels  
✔ Identify high-performing customer segments  
✔ Optimize marketing spend  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Businesses face challenges like:
- ❌ No unified view of campaign performance  
- ❌ Difficulty comparing ROI across channels  
- ❌ Poor visibility into customer behavior  
- ❌ Inefficient budget allocation  

---

## 💡 Solution
An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was built to:
- Integrate and clean marketing data  
- Create meaningful KPIs using DAX  
- Provide interactive filtering and drill-down  
- Deliver clear, insight-driven visuals  

---

## 📂 Dataset Details

| Category | Fields Included |
|----------|---------------|
| 📢 Campaign Info | Campaign Name, Channel, Type |
| 👥 Customer Data | Age, Income, Education |
| 💰 Financials | Cost, Revenue, Profit |
| 📈 Performance | Conversions, Clicks, Impressions |
| 📅 Time | Month, Year |

---

## 📊 KPI Metrics

| KPI | Formula / Meaning |
|-----|-----------------|
| 💰 Total Revenue | Sum of revenue generated |
| 💸 Total Cost | Total campaign spend |
| 📈 ROI | (Revenue - Cost) / Cost |
| 🎯 Conversion Rate | Conversions / Total Users |
| 👥 Customer Acquisition | New customers gained |
| 📢 Impressions | Total reach of campaigns |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Walkthrough

### 🔹 1. KPI Section
- Quick snapshot of overall performance  
- Helps decision-makers instantly assess results  

### 🔹 2. Channel Performance
- Compare ROI and conversions across channels  
- Identify top-performing platforms  

### 🔹 3. Trend Analysis
- Monthly and yearly performance tracking  
- Detect seasonality patterns  

### 🔹 4. Customer Segmentation
- Analyze performance by:
  - Age group  
  - Income level  
  - Education  

### 🔹 5. Campaign Comparison
- Evaluate which campaigns perform best  
- Spot underperforming campaigns  

---

## 🔍 Insights

✔ Social media campaigns generate higher engagement  
✔ Mid-age customer segment shows better conversion  
✔ High budget ≠ High ROI (efficiency matters)  
✔ Some campaigns require re-targeting strategies  

---

## 🎨 Design Strategy

### 🧠 UX Principles Used
- Minimal and clean layout  
- Insight-driven titles (not generic names)  
- Logical flow: KPI → Trends → Details  

### 🎯 Visual Design
- Consistent color palette  
- Proper spacing & alignment  
- Highlighting key metrics  

### ⚡ Interactivity
- Slicers for dynamic filtering  
- Cross-filtering between visuals  
- Drill-down capabilities
